Publications

The design and main ideas behind Haizea were presented in the following paper:

Combining Batch Execution and Leasing Using Virtual Machines, Borja Sotomayor, Kate Keahey, Ian Foster. ACM/IEEE International Symposium on High Performance Distributed Computing 2008 (HPDC 2008), Boston, MA. June 2008.
[pdf]  [slides]

The following short paper used a precursor to Haizea (and is, itself, a precursor to the above paper):

Enabling Cost-Effective Resource Leases with Virtual Machines, Borja Sotomayor, Kate Keahey, Ian Foster, Tim Freeman. Hot Topics session in ACM/IEEE International Symposium on High Performance Distributed Computing 2007 (HPDC 2007), Monterey Bay, CA (USA), June 27-29, 2007.
[pdf]

Borja Sotomayor's master's thesis also used a precursor to Haizea and describes some of the internal Haizea algorithms in much more detail than the above papers. Note, however, that there have been many changes to Haizea's algorithms since the thesis was written:

A Resource Management Model for VM-based Virtual Workspaces, Borja Sotomayor (MSc Thesis). University of Chicago, Department of Computer Science. Defended January 3rd, 2007, approved February 23rd, 2007.
[pdf]  [slides]
